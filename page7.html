Rhythm-Based FPS (Team Project)

In this project, I worked in a team of nine to create a rhythm-based FPS where the player deals more damage when shooting in sync with the music's beat. The team consisted of five Technical Artists and four Game Programmers.

The game was created completely from scratch, as we built our own engine in DirectX11, and then worked on the game for five months. During this time, we used the Scrum-method for our workflow, working in sprints and tracking our progress with a burndown chart.

My main responsibility was the system driving the skeletal animations of the enemies, but I also contributed on a couple of gameplay elements, shaders and assets. Further, I created the custom binary file-format containing the models and animations with another team member, as well as the importer and exporter for this format.

The purpose of this project is to show that I can:
- Work in a team
- Use Github for version control
- Follow agile methods such as Scrum to plan and develop games and tools
