A horse model sculptured in Blender, retopologized in Maya and textured in Substance Painter.

A. Retopologized mesh with texture and normal map. (5978 triangles)
B. Retopologized mesh with normal map baked from the high-poly mesh. (5978 triangles)
C. Retopologized mesh. (5978 triangles)
D. High-poly mesh. (346 720 triangles)

The topology, uv-map, normal-map and texture are displayed below.

The purpose of this project is to show that I can:
- Sculpt in Blender
- Bake normal maps from a high-poly mesh in Blender
- Retopologize a mesh in Maya
- Create clean, quad-based topology and edge flow
- Optimize poly counts
- Perform space-efficient UV-mapping in Maya
- Create textures in Substance Painter
