    3D Art Projects

    Garden

    A garden modeled in Maya, textured in Photoshop and rendered in Unreal Engine.
    In Unreal, the lantern's glow is programmed to flicker, and the river water to slowly flow forward. All textures are hand-painted, one of them is displayed below.

    Horse Model

    A rough horse model sculptured in Blender and then retopologized in Maya.
    1. Retopologized mesh with a normal map baked from the high-poly mesh. (3192 triangles)
    2. Retopologized mesh. (3192 triangles)
    3. High-poly mesh. (37 848 triangles)

    The difference between the low-poly and high-poly topology is shown in the image below, with the top horse being the low-poly model.
