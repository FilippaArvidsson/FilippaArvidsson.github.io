Freezable Water Shader

The Freezable Water Shader is a highly customizable shader with the ability to freeze and unfreeze in a certain pattern, given a black and white texture that determines the transition. Some of the adjustable properties include the water's refraction strength, flow speed and color. The properties of water and ice are set separately. The customization is shown further down under "Customization".

The shader was written in Unity in HLSL and works by refracting the world behind it based on a refraction strength and normal map. The freezing effect is achieved by blending with an ice texture depending on the freeze percentage and the transition texture, where the darkest color freezes first. Different freeze patterns are shown further down under "Freezing Patterns".

The purpose of this project is to show that I can:
- Write shaders in HLSL
- Utilize textures for special effects
- Create tiling normal-maps
- Create user-friendly interfaces for customization

Customization

The customization options and the three required textures.

When creating shaders it is very important to have user-friendliness in mind, in case they will be used by others. Hence, all options have descriptive names and are grouped under either water or ice, depending on which part of the material they influence.
